# Aviator Signal AI — Product Overview

> **Working title** — final app name to be confirmed during onboarding.

## What It Is

A mobile-first AI-powered signal intelligence web app (PWA, accessible on Android) that helps an internal team make data-informed decisions on Aviator game rounds. The app applies machine learning to historical round data to generate predictive signals — both for current state and near-future trend windows — while transparently tracking each signal's accuracy over time.

## Users & Audience

- **Primary users:** Internal team members (private, team-only access — not publicly available)
- **Access model:** Closed team tool; multi-member, shared access

## Core Features

1. **AI Signal Prediction** — Real-time signal output derived from AI models trained on historical Aviator round data. Surfaces both current-state signals and upcoming trend projections.
2. **Historical Signal Log** — Complete archive of past rounds and the signals they produced; filterable and reviewable for retrospective pattern analysis.
3. **Accuracy Tracker** — Per-signal and aggregate accuracy metrics showing prediction hit rates across rolling time windows. Every signal carries its own performance record.
4. **Trend Dashboard** — Visual summary of active signal trends, momentum windows, and pattern indicators — at a glance for the whole team.

## Positioning

A private intelligence layer that turns Aviator round data into actionable signals. Replaces manual guesswork with AI-driven pattern recognition; builds team confidence through transparent, auditable accuracy metrics.

## Platform

Mobile-first web app (PWA), optimised for Android browser use by internal team members.

## Brand & Tone

- Precise, data-forward, and authoritative
- Professional tool aesthetic — functional over decorative
- Trust is earned through transparency: every prediction carries its accuracy history
- Color direction: deep navy, electric blue / teal accents — signal-board aesthetic
